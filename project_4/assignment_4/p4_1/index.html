My favorite kind of music is hip hop, indie rock, and r&b. Each of those genres has a distinctive style as well as artists who create those styles. The thing about music is that it defines you as a person.

My favorite rock bands are the Black Keys, Red Hot Chili Peppers, Bloc Party, We Are Scientists, the Yeah Yeah Yeahs, and Best Coast. I like the Black Keys because they have a combination of blues, rock, and soul. Bloc Party, and the Yeah Yeah Yeahs, have distinctive indie rock sounds that no one has ever heard before.

My favorite hip hop artists are Kendrick Lamar, J.Cole, Lupe Fiasco, Childish Gambino, Mos Def, and Talib Kweli. I like all of those rappers because they're not just mainstream rappers, I consider them to be poets.
